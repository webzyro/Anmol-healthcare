Recognize the Symptoms

If you experience any of these common symptoms, it's important to consult with a medical professional for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan.

Chest Pain

Chest pain can be a symptom of a heart attack or other serious heart conditions. If you experience chest pain along with shortness of breath, nausea, or lightheadedness, seek immediate medical attention.

Learn More

Severe Headache

Severe headaches that do not respond to over-the-counter medication can be a sign of migraine, tension headaches, or other neurological conditions. If your headache is persistent, consult a healthcare provider.

Learn More

Shortness of Breath

Shortness of breath can indicate respiratory issues such as asthma, pneumonia, or other serious lung conditions. If it occurs suddenly or is accompanied by chest pain, seek medical attention immediately.

Learn More

Abdominal Pain

Abdominal pain can range from mild discomfort to severe pain. It may be a sign of indigestion, ulcers, or more serious conditions such as appendicitis. If the pain is persistent or severe, consult your doctor.

Learn More

Dizziness or Lightheadedness

Feeling dizzy or lightheaded could be a sign of dehydration, anemia, or a more serious issue like a stroke. If dizziness is frequent or accompanied by other symptoms, seek medical help.

Learn More

Nausea & Vomiting

Nausea and vomiting can be caused by various conditions including viral infections, food poisoning, or more severe issues like appendicitis or pregnancy complications. If persistent, consult a healthcare provider.
